How to stay safe from COVID19

Stay aware of the latest information on the COVID19 outbreak. Take care of your health and protect others too by doing the following.

i)Wash your hands frequently

Washing hand with Soap and water, or using alcohol-based hand rub will kill viruses on your hand.

ii)Maintain social distancing

Maintain atleast 1 meter distance between yourself and people who is coughing or sneezing.

iii)Avoid touching your eyes,nose, and mouth unnecessarily

Hands touch many surfaces and can pick up viruses. Once contaminated, hands can transfer the virus to your eyes,nose or mouth. From ther it can enter your body and can make you sick.

iv)Practice respiratory hygiene

Cover your mouth and nose with your bent elbow or tissue when you cough or sneeze.Use mask if you are having symptoms.

v)Stay informed and seek medical care if necessary

Stay informed about the latest developments in about the COVID-19. If you have fever,cough and difficulty in breathing, seek medical care early.
